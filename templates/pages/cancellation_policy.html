Cancellation & Refund Policy

Understanding your rights and our policies for subscription cancellations and refunds

Subscription Cancellation

At Colorify, we understand that circumstances change. You have the right to cancel your subscription at any time. Our cancellation process is designed to be simple and transparent, ensuring you maintain control over your account and billing.

To cancel your subscription, you can do so through your account dashboard, or by contacting our customer support team. Once cancelled, you'll continue to have access to Colorify's services until the end of your current billing period. No further charges will be applied to your account.

Refund Policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you're not completely satisfied with Colorify's AI-powered color intelligence platform within the first 30 days of your subscription, we'll provide a full refund of your subscription fee.

For existing subscribers, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We consider factors such as service usage, technical issues, and customer satisfaction. Partial refunds may be available for unused portions of annual subscriptions if cancelled mid-term.

Please note that refunds are processed within 5-10 business days and will be credited back to your original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

Cancellation Process

To cancel your Colorify subscription, follow these simple steps:

1

Log into your Colorify account dashboard

2

Navigate to the subscription management section

3

Click on "Cancel Subscription" and confirm your decision

4

Receive confirmation email with cancellation details
